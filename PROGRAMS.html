SOCCER

Technical Skills:

Ball control, dribbling, passing, receiving, shooting, heading, tackling, first touch.

Tactical Understanding:

Formations, defensive shape, attacking patterns, pressing, build-up play, set pieces (corners, free kicks)

Psychological/Mental:

Decision-making, focus, game awareness, confidence, resilience.

Recovery & Flexibility:

Stretching, cool-downs, injury prevention, rest.

BASKETBALL

Dribbling:

Ball security (functional) and creating moves (shot creation, crossovers).

Shooting:

Layups, jump shots (off dribble, spot-up), free throws, and deep shots.

Passing:

PnR (pick-and-roll), post passing, skip passes, and creating opportunities (passing gravity).

Finishing:

Scoring near the basket (layups, floaters, dunks)

Defense:

On-ball (staying with opponent), off-ball (positioning), stealing, blocking, and boxing out.

Rebounding:

Securing missed shots (offensive & defensive). 

SPEED AND AGILITY:

Reaction Ability:

Responding quickly to a stimulus.

Movement Speed:

How fast you can move your body.

Acceleration:

Building up speed from a standstill.

Linear Speed:

Moving in a straight line (sprinting).

Lateral Speed:

Moving side-to-side (shuffling).

Speed Endurace

Maintaining speed over time.
